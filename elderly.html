SAY HELLO
Elder.ly is a web video chat application designed and built at PennApps XVI for elderly users to connect with one another quickly and easily. We recognize that old age is often a lonely time, and Elder.ly aims to fill the space that currently exists in tech for the elderly.

Backend: Node Frontend: HTML5, raw CSS3, Javascript Frameworks: SimpleWebRTC
